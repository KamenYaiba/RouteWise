About Us

RouteWise is a data-driven traffic monitoring web app that helps users optimize their travel times by automatically collecting and analyzing real-time traffic data. By integrating Google Maps, the app tracks frequently used routes, stores ETA trends, and provides smart travel insights to help users avoid congestion.

Whether you're a daily commuter or a business needing precise travel estimates, Smart Traffic makes navigation efficient, time-saving, and hassle-free.
